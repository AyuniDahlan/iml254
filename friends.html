CRAZY PEOPLE

We have a group of friends called "crazy people." This group was founded in 2018 because we became close that year. It all started with the four of us being in the same class in high school. We made many beautiful memories together during that time. When our school days ended, the four of us went our separate ways: Ayuni and Azu, also known as Zubaidah, are still pursuing diploma programs at different UiTM campuses in different states. Meanwhile, Zahirah and Arissa have continued their studies at the bachelor's level at different universities. Despite going our separate ways, we make it a point to meet and spend time together whenever we get long breaks and our schedules align. These reunions are precious to us, and when I am with them, I feel like time flies and it envies us for the joy we share.
